## Item #1: Website Revenue Audit Checklist
**Perceived Value:** $97

### What It Is
A step-by-step checklist the business owner walks through to evaluate whether their website is set up to generate revenue. Designed so a non-technical person can complete it in 20 minutes.

### Contents
**Section A — First Impressions (Score 1-5 each)**
- [ ] Does your homepage clearly state what you do within 5 seconds?
- [ ] Is there a clear call-to-action above the fold?
- [ ] Does your site load in under 3 seconds? (Test at: https://pagespeed.web.dev)
- [ ] Is your phone number/contact info visible without scrolling?
- [ ] Does the design look current (not dated)?

**Section B — Mobile Experience**
- [ ] Does your site display correctly on a phone?
- [ ] Are buttons large enough to tap with a thumb?
- [ ] Does text read without zooming?
- [ ] Do forms work on mobile?
- [ ] Does the menu work on mobile?

**Section C — Trust & Credibility**
- [ ] Do you have customer testimonials visible?
- [ ] Is your About page complete with real photos?
- [ ] Do you display any certifications, awards, or affiliations?
- [ ] Is your address/location listed?
- [ ] Do you have a privacy policy and terms page?

**Section D — Lead Capture**
- [ ] Do you have at least one lead capture form?
- [ ] Do you offer something of value in exchange for an email?
- [ ] Is there a clear next step for visitors (call, form, buy)?
- [ ] Do you have a thank-you/confirmation page?
- [ ] Are you tracking form submissions?

**Section E — SEO Basics**
- [ ] Does each page have a unique title tag?
- [ ] Does each page have a meta description?
- [ ] Are your images optimized (compressed, alt tags)?
- [ ] Is your site on HTTPS (SSL)?
- [ ] Is your Google Business Profile claimed and updated?

**Scoring:**
- 20-25: Your site is solid — fine-tuning will boost results
- 15-19: There are gaps costing you money — prioritize the unchecked items
- Below 15: Your website needs serious attention — consider professional help

## Item #4: Website Speed Optimization Checklist
**Perceived Value:** $47

### What It Is
A technical checklist for improving website load speed. Includes both DIY fixes and items that may need a developer.

### Contents

**DIY Fixes (No Developer Needed):**
- [ ] Compress images before uploading (use https://tinypng.com — free)
- [ ] Delete unused plugins
- [ ] Delete unused themes (keep only your active theme + one default)
- [ ] Install a caching plugin (WP Super Cache or LiteSpeed Cache — free)
- [ ] Enable lazy loading for images (most themes have this built in)
- [ ] Remove unused widgets and sidebar items

**Developer-Level Fixes:**
- [ ] Enable GZIP compression on server
- [ ] Minify CSS and JavaScript files
- [ ] Implement browser caching headers
- [ ] Use a CDN (Cloudflare free tier)
- [ ] Optimize database (remove post revisions, spam comments, transients)
- [ ] Evaluate hosting — shared hosting is often the bottleneck

**How to Test:**
1. Go to https://pagespeed.web.dev
2. Enter your website URL
3. Check both Mobile and Desktop scores
4. Target: 70+ on mobile, 85+ on desktop
