## Copy Tone Guidelines

The homepage copy should be:
- **Julia's voice** — warm, artistic, confident but approachable
- **Visual-first** — let the artwork do the talking, copy supports it
- **Benefit-focused** — what the client gets, not a lecture on marketing theory
- **Concise** — no "Dear Wise Business Owner" letters
- **Modern** — 2026 language, not 2016 direct mail

Avoid:
- "98% of so called graphic artists" — negative/combative
- "Dear Wise Business Owner" — dated direct mail format
- Long marketing theory essays on the homepage
- Ed's voice dominating Julia's site (save that for blog posts)
- "and much, much more" — vague filler
