## Current Problem
The services page is a bare numbered list:
```
1. Graphic Design - brochures, flyers, posters, menus, etc..
2. Package/Product Design
3. Illustration/Print Design
4. Web Design
5. Marketing
```

No descriptions, no portfolio examples, no pricing guidance, no CTAs. For a graphic design business, this is the most critical page after the homepage.

## Pricing Guidance

The current FAQ mentions a 3-phase pricing model (Research → Sketch → Design). This is good but should be simplified for the services page:

```
Pricing note (add to each service or as a separate section):

"Every project is unique, so I provide custom quotes based on your specific
needs. Most projects fall in these ranges:

• Logo design: starting at $XXX
• Business card design: starting at $XXX
• Brochure/flyer: starting at $XXX
• Custom illustration: starting at $XXX

Request a free quote for an exact price on your project."
```

**Note:** Julia and Ed need to fill in actual pricing. Even ballpark ranges help — visitors who see no pricing often assume it's too expensive and leave.

## Design Notes
- Each service section should have a portfolio image or gallery
- Use a consistent card/section layout
- Mobile-friendly — services should stack vertically on phones
- Each service has its own CTA (don't make people scroll to the bottom)
- Consider adding a "Most Popular" badge on the Logo & Brand Identity service
